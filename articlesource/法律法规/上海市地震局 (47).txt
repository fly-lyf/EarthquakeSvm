标准名称：</strong><span>GB/T 23648</span>－<span>2009</span>《社区志愿者地震应急与救援工作指南》</div>
<div><strong>英文名称：</strong><span>Guideline of earthquake emergency response and rescue for community volunteer</span></div>
<div><strong>发布日期：</strong><span>2009</span>年<span>04</span>月<span>17</span>日</div>
