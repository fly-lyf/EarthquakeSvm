<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; TEXT-INDENT: 28pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 30pt; mso-line-height-rule: exactly; mso-char-indent-count: 2.0"><SPAN style="FONT-SIZE: 14pt; FONT-FAMILY: 仿宋_GB2312">根据中国数字地震观测网络项目实施计划安排，定于<?xml:namespace prefix = st1 ns = "urn:schemas-microsoft-com:office:smarttags" /><st1:chsdate w:st="on" IsROCDate="False" IsLunarDate="False" Day="23" Month="10" Year="2006"><SPAN lang=EN-US>2006</SPAN>年<SPAN lang=EN-US>10</SPAN>月<SPAN lang=EN-US>23</SPAN>日起</st1:chsdate>在北京分批举办<SPAN lang=EN-US>Oracle</SPAN>数据库和中间件培训班。为保证本次及以后的培训效果，稳定参加培训人员队伍，切实提高《中国数字地震观测网络》项目建设和运行维护人员的技术水平。经研究，决定：<SPAN lang=EN-US><o:p></o:p></SPAN></SPAN></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; TEXT-INDENT: 28pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 30pt; mso-line-height-rule: exactly; mso-char-indent-count: 2.0"><SPAN lang=EN-US style="FONT-SIZE: 14pt; FONT-FAMILY: 仿宋_GB2312">1</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-SIZE: 14pt; FONT-FAMILY: 仿宋_GB2312">．自本期培训班起，各单位须明确将信息网络技术人员分为两类：一类为数据库和信息服务管理员，一类为网络管理员，并需保证参加人员的相对稳定性。请各单位做好技术人员的分工。此后的培训将明确要求相应的管理员参加。<SPAN lang=EN-US><o:p></o:p></SPAN></SPAN></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; TEXT-INDENT: 28pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 30pt; mso-line-height-rule: exactly; mso-char-indent-count: 2.0"><SPAN lang=EN-US style="FONT-SIZE: 14pt; FONT-FAMILY: 仿宋_GB2312">2</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-SIZE: 14pt; FONT-FAMILY: 仿宋_GB2312">．建立长效的培训考核机制。培训结束后，将组织有关专家对参加培训人员进行笔试或上机考核，考核未通过者将以书面形式反馈给参加培训人员所在单位。<SPAN lang=EN-US><o:p></o:p></SPAN></SPAN></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; TEXT-INDENT: 28pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 30pt; mso-line-height-rule: exactly; mso-char-indent-count: 2.0"><SPAN lang=EN-US style="FONT-SIZE: 14pt; FONT-FAMILY: 仿宋_GB2312">3</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-SIZE: 14pt; FONT-FAMILY: 仿宋_GB2312">．从本期培训起，请各单位尽量选派青年技术骨干参加。<SPAN lang=EN-US><o:p></o:p></SPAN></SPAN></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; TEXT-INDENT: 28pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 30pt; mso-line-height-rule: exactly; mso-char-indent-count: 2.0"><SPAN style="FONT-SIZE: 14pt; FONT-FAMILY: 仿宋_GB2312">本期培训班的有关安排及要求如下：<SPAN lang=EN-US><o:p></o:p></SPAN></SPAN></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; TEXT-INDENT: 28pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 30pt; mso-line-height-rule: exactly; mso-char-indent-count: 2.0; mso-outline-level: 1"><SPAN style="FONT-SIZE: 14pt; FONT-FAMILY: 黑体">一、培训内容<SPAN lang=EN-US><o:p></o:p></SPAN></SPAN></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; TEXT-INDENT: 28pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 30pt; mso-line-height-rule: exactly; mso-char-indent-count: 2.0"><SPAN lang=EN-US style="FONT-SIZE: 14pt; FONT-FAMILY: 仿宋_GB2312">1</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-SIZE: 14pt; FONT-FAMILY: 仿宋_GB2312">．<SPAN lang=EN-US>Oracle</SPAN>初级培训班：<SPAN lang=EN-US>Oracle</SPAN>创建数据库；存储结构；用户管理和数据管理；网络配置；备份及恢复试验以及上机实习等。共计五天。<SPAN lang=EN-US><o:p></o:p></SPAN></SPAN></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; TEXT-INDENT: 28pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 30pt; mso-line-height-rule: exactly; mso-char-indent-count: 2.0"><SPAN lang=EN-US style="FONT-SIZE: 14pt; FONT-FAMILY: 仿宋_GB2312">2</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-SIZE: 14pt; FONT-FAMILY: 仿宋_GB2312">．<SPAN lang=EN-US>Oracle</SPAN>高级培训班：<SPAN lang=EN-US>Oracle</SPAN>创建数据库；存储结构；用户管理和数据管理；网络配置；备份及恢复试验<SPAN lang=EN-US>;Oracle rman</SPAN>使用介绍<SPAN lang=EN-US>;</SPAN>分布式交易；数据库恢复及试验<SPAN lang=EN-US>;Oracle</SPAN>资源管理以及上机实习等。分两期举办，每期两个班，各十天。<SPAN lang=EN-US><o:p></o:p></SPAN></SPAN></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; TEXT-INDENT: 28pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 30pt; mso-line-height-rule: exactly; mso-char-indent-count: 2.0"><SPAN lang=EN-US style="FONT-SIZE: 14pt; FONT-FAMILY: 仿宋_GB2312">3</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-SIZE: 14pt; FONT-FAMILY: 仿宋_GB2312">．中间件培训班：<SPAN lang=EN-US>BEA WebLogic Server 9.0:</SPAN>系统管理；<SPAN lang=EN-US>BEA</SPAN>开发培训课程等，共计六天。该培训班将紧接着第二期<SPAN lang=EN-US>Oracle</SPAN>高级培训班举办。<SPAN lang=EN-US><o:p></o:p></SPAN></SPAN></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; TEXT-INDENT: 28pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 30pt; mso-line-height-rule: exactly; mso-char-indent-count: 2.0; mso-outline-level: 1"><SPAN style="FONT-SIZE: 14pt; FONT-FAMILY: 黑体">二、主要要求<SPAN lang=EN-US><o:p></o:p></SPAN></SPAN></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; TEXT-INDENT: 28pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 30pt; mso-line-height-rule: exactly; mso-char-indent-count: 2.0"><SPAN lang=EN-US style="FONT-SIZE: 14pt; FONT-FAMILY: 仿宋_GB2312">1</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-SIZE: 14pt; FONT-FAMILY: 仿宋_GB2312">．本期培训参加者须为数据库和信息服务管理员。<SPAN lang=EN-US><o:p></o:p></SPAN></SPAN></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; TEXT-INDENT: 28pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 30pt; mso-line-height-rule: exactly; mso-char-indent-count: 2.0"><SPAN lang=EN-US style="FONT-SIZE: 14pt; FONT-FAMILY: 仿宋_GB2312">2</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-SIZE: 14pt; FONT-FAMILY: 仿宋_GB2312">．各单位参加各培训的人数严格限定在“<SPAN lang=EN-US>Oracle</SPAN>数据库、中间件采购合同”约定的范围内。<SPAN lang=EN-US><o:p></o:p></SPAN></SPAN></P>
