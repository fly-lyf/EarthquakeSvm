<p style="text-indent:23.5pt;mso-char-indent-count:1.96;
line-height:150%" class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-size:12.0pt;line-height:150%;
font-family:宋体;mso-ascii-theme-font:minor-fareast;mso-fareast-theme-font:minor-fareast;
mso-hansi-theme-font:minor-fareast">26</span><span style="font-size:12.0pt;
line-height:150%;font-family:宋体;mso-ascii-theme-font:minor-fareast;mso-fareast-theme-font:
minor-fareast;mso-hansi-theme-font:minor-fareast">日，国家地震灾害紧急救援队派出<span lang="EN-US">3</span>支救援分队沿芦山县和天全县方向全力开展搜索排查。</span></p>
<p style="text-indent:24.0pt;mso-char-indent-count:2.0;
line-height:150%" class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-size:12.0pt;line-height:150%;font-family:
宋体;mso-ascii-theme-font:minor-fareast;mso-fareast-theme-font:minor-fareast;
mso-hansi-theme-font:minor-fareast">第一分队<span lang="EN-US">28</span>人沿芦山县新华乡和天全县思延乡搜索排查。在芦山县新华乡落改村排查危房<span lang="EN-US">24</span>户，治疗烧伤群众<span lang="EN-US">1</span>人，协助受灾民众搭建帐篷<span lang="EN-US">14</span>顶，搬运物资<span lang="EN-US">50</span>余件。在天全县思延乡，搜索排查<span lang="EN-US">1</span>个行政村、<span lang="EN-US">1</span>个铁合金工厂、<span lang="EN-US">1</span>个小型水电站，排查受损民宅<span lang="EN-US">13</span>户、危房<span lang="EN-US">46</span>间、厂房<span lang="EN-US">16</span>间，协助受灾民众搬运物品<span lang="EN-US">388</span>件。</span></p>
<p style="text-indent:24.0pt;mso-char-indent-count:2.0;
line-height:150%" class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-size:12.0pt;line-height:150%;font-family:
宋体;mso-ascii-theme-font:minor-fareast;mso-fareast-theme-font:minor-fareast;
mso-hansi-theme-font:minor-fareast">第二分队<span lang="EN-US">28</span>人沿芦山县升隆乡、清源乡搜索排查。在芦山县升隆乡高车村搜索和排查了<span lang="EN-US">13</span>户<span lang="EN-US">42</span>间房屋，帮助村民转移粮食<span lang="EN-US">12</span>袋，家电<span lang="EN-US">6</span>件套。在清源乡搜索排查了<span lang="EN-US">15</span>户<span lang="EN-US">47</span>间房屋，协助灾民搭建帐篷<span lang="EN-US">8</span>顶，提供食品<span lang="EN-US">6</span>箱，饮用水<span lang="EN-US">6</span>件。</span></p>
<p style="text-indent:24.0pt;mso-char-indent-count:2.0;
line-height:150%" class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-size:12.0pt;line-height:150%;font-family:
宋体;mso-ascii-theme-font:minor-fareast;mso-fareast-theme-font:minor-fareast;
mso-hansi-theme-font:minor-fareast">第三分队<span lang="EN-US">80</span>人沿芦山县城及周边，重点对县城西部和东南部进行搜索排查。分队分成两个小组，第一小组<span lang="EN-US">66</span>人，搜索排查<span lang="EN-US">52</span>栋<span lang="EN-US">173</span>间房屋，协助居民转移物品<span lang="EN-US">320</span>余件套，并对居民进行防震减灾知识宣传。第二小组<span lang="EN-US">14</span>人，在营地附近的先锋村和火炬村开展工作，重点搜索排查先锋村<span lang="EN-US">8</span>户受损严重民宅，协助村民转移物资<span lang="EN-US">58</span>件套，并发放了食物和水，同时对伤亡人员的家属进行了心理疏导。</span></p>
<p style="text-indent:24.0pt;mso-char-indent-count:2.0;
line-height:150%" class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-size:12.0pt;line-height:150%;font-family:
宋体;mso-ascii-theme-font:minor-fareast;mso-fareast-theme-font:minor-fareast;
mso-hansi-theme-font:minor-fareast">医疗救护方面，在清仁乡前坝组安置区医疗点，全天共接诊<span lang="EN-US">70</span>余人次，巡诊<span lang="EN-US">16</span>人次；对该居民安置区域进行了全面洗消；通过互动和儿童集体游戏的方式进行心理干预；向县医院卫生所捐赠了部分药品。<span lang="EN-US">10</span>名医疗队员共巡诊灾民<span lang="EN-US">30</span>余人次。</span></p>
