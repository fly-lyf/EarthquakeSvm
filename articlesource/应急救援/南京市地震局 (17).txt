</span></span>言</a>&nbsp;</p><p style="text-indent: 21pt" class="a4">　　<span>为了应对地震突发事件，防御与减轻地震灾害，科学合理地建设地震应急避难场所，为居民提供应急避险空间，快速有序<span class="msoIns"><ins datetime="2008-10-27T17:04" lang="EN-US">GB 18208.2</span>－<span lang="EN-US">2001<span>&nbsp; </span></span>地震现场工作<span lang="EN-US"><span>&nbsp; </span></span>第<span lang="EN-US"><span class="msoIns"><ins datetime="2008-10-27T17:07" cite="mailto:ibm">2</ins></span></span>部分：建筑物安全鉴定</span>&nbsp;</p><p style="text-indent: 21pt" class="a4">　　<span><span lang="EN-US">GB 18306</span>－<span lang="EN-US">2001<span>&nbsp; </span></span>中国地震动<span class="msoIns"><ins datetime="2008-10-27T17:07" cite="mailto:ibm">参数</ins></span>区划参数图</span>&nbsp;</p><p style="text-indent: 21pt" class="a4">　　<span><span lang="EN-US">JGJ 50</span>－</span><span><span lang="EN-US">2001 </span>城市道路和建筑物无障碍设计规范</span>&nbsp;</p><p style="text-indent: 0cm; margin: 7.8pt 0cm" class="a5">　　<span><span lang="EN-US"><span>3　</span></span>术语和定义</span>&nbsp;</p><p style="text-indent: 21pt" class="a4">　　<span><span>下列术语和定义适用于本标准。</span></span>&nbsp;</p><p style="text-indent: 0cm; margin-left: 0cm" class="a6">　　<span><span style="font-family: 黑体" lang="EN-US"><span>3.1　</span></span><span lang="EN-US/">&nbsp;</span></span></p><span><span lang="EN-US/"><p>　　<span>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; </span></p><p style="text-indent: 21pt" class="a4">　　<span>为应对地震等突发事件，经规划、建设，具有应急避难生活服务设施，可供居民紧急疏散、临时生活的安全场所。</span>&nbsp;</p><p style="text-indent: 0cm; margin-left: 0cm" class="a6">　　<span><span style="font-family: 黑体" lang="EN-US"><span>3.2　</span></span><span lang="EN-US/">&nbsp;</span></span></p><span><span lang="EN-US/"><p>　　<span>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; </span></p><p style="text-indent: 21pt" class="MsoNormal">　　<span><span style="font-family: 宋体">为保障避难人员基本生活需求，而应设置的配套设施。包括：救灾帐篷、简易活动房屋，医疗救护和卫生防疫设施，应急供水设施，应急供电设施，应急排污设施，应急厕所，应急垃圾储运设施，应急通道，应急标识等。</span></span>&nbsp;</p><p style="text-indent: 0cm; margin-left: 0cm" class="a6">　　<span><span style="font-family: 黑体" lang="EN-US"><span>3.3　</span></span><span lang="EN-US/">&nbsp;</span></span></p><span><span lang="EN-US/"><p>　　<span>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; </span></p><p style="text-indent: 21pt" class="MsoNormal">　　<span><span style="font-family: 宋体">为改善避难人员生活条件，在基本设施的基础上应增设的配套设施。包括：应急消防设施，应急物资储备设施，应急指挥管理设施等。</span></span>&nbsp;</p><p style="text-indent: 0cm; margin-left: 0cm" class="a6">　　<span><span style="font-family: 黑体" lang="EN-US"><span>3.4　</span></span><span lang="EN-US/">&nbsp;</span></span></p><span><span lang="EN-US/"><p>　　<span>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; </span></p><p style="text-indent: 21pt" class="MsoNormal">　　<span><span style="font-family: 宋体">为提高避难人员<span class="msoIns"><ins datetime="2008-10-27T17:14" cite="mailto:ibm">的</ins></span>生活条件，在已有的基本<span class="msoIns"><ins datetime="2008-10-27T17:15" cite="mailto:ibm">设施</ins></span>、一般设施的基础上，应增设的配套设施。包括：应急停车场，应急停机坪，应急洗浴设施，应急通风设施，应急功能介绍设施等。</span></span>&nbsp;</p><p style="text-indent: 0cm; margin: 7.8pt 0cm" class="a5">　　<span lang="EN-US"><span>4　</span></span>分类</p><p style="text-indent: 21pt" class="a4">　　地震应急避难场所分为以下三类：</p><p style="text-indent: 21pt" class="a4">　　——Ⅰ类地震应急避难场所：具备综合设施<span class="msoIns"><ins datetime="2008-10-27T17:16" cite="mailto:ibm">配置</ins></span>，可安置受助人员<span lang="EN-US">30 d</span>以上；</p><p style="text-indent: 21pt" class="a4">　　——Ⅱ类地震应急避难场所：具备一般设施<span class="msoIns"><ins datetime="2008-10-27T17:16" cite="mailto:ibm">配置</ins></span>，可安置受助人员<span lang="EN-US">10 d</span><span>～</span><span lang="EN-US">30 d</span>；</p><p style="text-indent: 21pt" class="a4">　　——Ⅲ类地震应急避难场所：具备基本设施<span class="msoIns"><ins datetime="2008-10-27T17:16" cite="mailto:ibm">配置</ins></span>，可安置受助人员<span lang="EN-US">10 d</span>以内。</p><p style="text-indent: 0cm; margin: 7.8pt 0cm" class="a5">　　<span lang="EN-US"><span>5　</span></span>场址要求</p><p style="text-indent: 0cm; margin-left: 0cm" class="a6">　　<span style="font-family: 黑体" lang="EN-US"><span>5.1　</span></span><span style="font-family: 黑体">场址选择</span>&nbsp;</p><p style="text-indent: 15.75pt" class="a4">　　下列场址可选作地震应急避难场所：</p><p style="text-indent: 15.35pt" class="a4">　　——公园（不包括动物园和公园内的文物古迹保护区域）；</p><p style="text-indent: 15.35pt" class="a4">　　——绿地；</p><p style="text-indent: 15.35pt" class="a4">　　——广场；</p><p style="text-indent: 15.35pt" class="a4">　　——体育场；</p><p style="text-indent: 15.35pt" class="a4">　　——室内公共的场、馆、所。</p><p style="text-indent: 0cm; margin-left: 0cm" class="a6">　　<span style="font-family: 黑体" lang="EN-US"><span>5.2　</span></span><span style="font-family: 黑体">安全性要求</span>&nbsp;</p><p style="text-indent: 0cm; margin-left: 0cm" class="a7">　　<span style="font-family: 黑体" lang="EN-US"><span>5.2.1　</span></span><span style="font-family: 黑体">应避开地震断裂带，洪涝、山体滑坡、泥石流等自然灾害易发生地段。</span>&nbsp;</p><p style="text-indent: 0cm; margin-left: 0cm" class="a7">　　<span style="font-family: 黑体" lang="EN-US"><span>5.2.2　</span></span><span style="font-family: 黑体">应选择地势较为平坦空旷且地势略高，易于排水，适宜搭建帐篷的地形。</span>&nbsp;</p><p style="text-indent: 0cm; margin-left: 0cm" class="a7">　　<span style="font-family: 黑体" lang="EN-US"><span>5.2.3　</span></span><span style="font-family: 黑体">应选择有毒气体储放地、易燃易爆物或核放射物储放地、高压输变电线路等设施对人身安全可能产生影响的范围之外。</span>&nbsp;</p><p style="text-indent: 0cm; margin-left: 0cm" class="a7">　　<span style="font-family: 黑体" lang="EN-US"><span>5.2.4　</span></span><span style="font-family: 黑体">应选择在高层建筑物、高耸构筑物的垮塌范围距离之外。</span>&nbsp;</p><p style="text-indent: 0cm" class="a4">　　<span lang="EN-US">5.2.5</span><span lang="EN-US"> </span>选择室内公共的场、馆、所作为地震应急避难场所或作为地震应急避难场所配套设施用房的，<span>应达到当地抗震设防要求，并在地震发生后依</span>照<span lang="EN-US">GB 18208.2</span>－<span lang="EN-US">2001</span>进行建筑物的安全鉴定，鉴定合格后方可启用。</p><p style="text-indent: 0cm; margin-left: 0cm" class="a6">　　<span style="font-family: 黑体" lang="EN-US"><span>5.3　</span></span><span style="font-family: 黑体">可通达性要求</span>&nbsp;</p><p style="text-indent: 21pt" class="a4">　　应急避难场所应有方向不同的两条以上与外界相通的疏散道路。</p><p style="text-indent: 0cm; margin-left: 0cm" class="a6">　　<span style="font-family: 黑体" lang="EN-US"><span>5.4　</span></span><span style="font-family: 黑体">面积要求</span>&nbsp;</p><p style="text-indent: 21pt" class="a4">　　场址有效面积宜大于<span lang="EN-US">2000 m<sup>2</sup></span>。</p><p style="text-indent: 21pt" class="a4">　　人均居住面积应大于<span lang="EN-US">1.5 m<sup>2</sup></span>。</p><p style="text-indent: 0cm; margin: 7.8pt 0cm" class="a5">　　<span lang="EN-US"><span>6　</span></span>设施配置</p><p style="text-indent: 0cm; margin-left: 0cm" class="a6">　　<span style="font-family: 黑体" lang="EN-US"><span>6.1　</span></span><span lang="EN-US"><span>&nbsp; </span></span><span style="font-family: 宋体">基本设施配置</span><span lang="EN-US"><span>&nbsp; </span></span>&nbsp;</p><p style="text-indent: 0cm; margin-left: 0cm" class="a7">　　<span style="font-family: 黑体" lang="EN-US"><span>6.1.1　</span></span><span style="font-family: 黑体">应急篷宿区设施</span>&nbsp;</p><p style="text-indent: 21pt" class="a4">　　应设置满足应急生活需要的帐篷、活动简易房等临时用房。</p><p style="text-indent: 0cm; margin-left: 0cm" class="a7">　　<span style="font-family: 黑体" lang="EN-US"><span>6.1.2　</span></span><span style="font-family: 黑体">医疗救护与卫生防疫设施</span>&nbsp;</p><p style="text-indent: 21pt" class="a4">　　<span>应设有临时或固定的用于紧急处置的医疗救护与卫生防疫设施。</span>&nbsp;</p><p style="text-indent: 0cm; margin-left: 0cm" class="a7">　　<span style="font-family: 黑体" lang="EN-US"><span>6.1.3　</span></span><span style="font-family: 黑体">应急供水设施</span>&nbsp;</p><p style="text-indent: 21pt" class="a4">　　可选择设置供水管网，供水车、蓄水池、水井、机井等两种以上供水设施，并根据所选设施<span>和当地水质配置用于净化自然水体成为直接饮用水的净化设备。</span>&nbsp;</p><p style="text-indent: 21pt" class="a4">　　每<span lang="EN-US">100</span>人应至少设一个水龙头，<span lang="EN-US">250</span>人应至少设一处饮水处。<span>生活饮用水水质应达到<span lang="EN-US">GB 5749</span>－<span lang="EN-US">2006</span>规定的要求。</span>&nbsp;</p><p style="text-indent: 0cm; margin-left: 0cm" class="a7">　　<span style="font-family: 黑体" lang="EN-US"><span>6.1.4　</span></span><span style="font-family: 黑体">应急供电设施</span>&nbsp;</p><p style="text-indent: 21pt" class="a4">　　应设置<span>保障照明、医疗、通讯用电的</span>具有多路电网供电系统或太阳能供电系统，或配置<span>可移动</span>发电机应急供电设施。</p><p style="text-indent: 21pt; margin-left: 0cm" class="a6">　　<span style="font-family: 黑体">供、发电设施应具备防触电、防雷击保护措施。</span>&nbsp;</p><p style="text-indent: 0cm; margin-left: 0cm" class="a7">　　<span style="font-family: 黑体" lang="EN-US"><span>6.1.5　</span></span><span style="font-family: 黑体">应急排污系统</span>&nbsp;</p><p style="text-indent: 21pt" class="a4">　　<span>应设置满足应急生活需要和避免造成环境污染的排放管线、简易污水处理设施。</span>&nbsp;</p><p style="text-indent: 21pt" class="a4">　　<span>应急排污系统应与市政管道相连接或设立独立排污系统。</span>&nbsp;</p><p style="text-indent: 0cm; margin-left: 0cm" class="a7">　　<span style="font-family: 黑体" lang="EN-US"><span>6.1.6　</span></span><span style="font-family: 黑体">应急厕所</span><span lang="EN-US"><span>&nbsp; </span></span>&nbsp;</p><p style="text-indent: 21pt; margin-left: 0cm; tab-stops: 21.0pt" class="af0">　　应设置满足应急生活需要的暗坑式厕所或移动式厕所。</p><p style="text-indent: 21pt" class="a4">　　应急厕所之间距离应小于<span lang="EN-US">100 m</span>，且位于应急避难场所下风向设置。距离篷宿区<span lang="EN-US">30 m</span><span>～</span><span lang="EN-US">50 m</span>。</p><p style="text-indent: 21pt" class="a4">　　暗坑式厕所应具备水冲能力，并附设或单独设置化粪池。</p><p style="text-indent: 0cm; margin-left: 0cm" class="a7">　　<span style="font-family: 黑体" lang="EN-US"><span>6.1.7　</span></span><span style="font-family: 黑体">应急垃圾储运设施</span>&nbsp;</p><p style="text-indent: 21pt; margin-left: 0cm; tab-stops: 21.0pt" class="af0">　　应设置满足应急生活需要的可移动的垃圾、废弃物分类储运设施。</p><p style="text-indent: 21pt; margin-left: 0cm; tab-stops: 21.0pt" class="af0">　　应急垃圾储运设施距离应急<span class="msoIns"><ins datetime="2008-10-27T17:27" cite="mailto:ibm">篷</ins></span>宿区应大于<span lang="EN-US">5</span>米，且位于应急避难场所下风向设置。</p><p style="text-indent: 0cm; margin-left: 0cm" class="a7">　　<span style="font-family: 黑体" lang="EN-US"><span>6.1.8　</span></span><span style="font-family: 黑体">应急通道</span>&nbsp;</p><p style="text-indent: 20.25pt" class="a4">　　篷宿区周边和<span>场所内</span>要按照防火、卫生防疫要求设置通道。</p><p style="text-indent: 0cm; margin-left: 0cm" class="a7">　　<span style="font-family: 黑体" lang="EN-US"><span>6.1.9　</span></span><span style="font-family: 黑体">应急标<span class="msoIns"><ins datetime="2008-10-27T17:31" cite="mailto:ibm">志</ins></span></span><span lang="EN-US" />&nbsp;</p><p style="text-indent: 21pt" class="a4">　　<span>地震应急避难场所及周边应设置避难场所标识、人员疏导标<span class="msoIns"><ins datetime="2008-10-27T17:29" cite="mailto:ibm">志</ins></span>和应急避难功能分区标识。</span>&nbsp;</p><p style="text-indent: -32.25pt; margin-left: 32.25pt; tab-stops: list 32.25pt" class="a6">　　<span style="font-family: 黑体" lang="EN-US"><span>6.2<span style="font: 7pt 'Times New Roman'">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; </span></span></span><span style="font-family: 黑体" lang="EN-US"><span>&nbsp;&nbsp;</span></span><span style="font-family: 黑体">一般设施配置</span>&nbsp;</p><p style="text-indent: 21pt" class="a4">　　在基本设施的基础上增加以下设施。</p><p style="text-indent: -36pt; margin-left: 36pt; tab-stops: list 36.0pt" class="a7">　　<span lang="EN-US"><span>6.2.1<span style="font: 7pt 'Times New Roman'">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; </span></span></span><span style="font-family: 黑体">应急消防设施</span>&nbsp;</p><p style="text-indent: 20.25pt" class="a4">　　<span>应急期间应急篷宿区应配置灭火工具或器材设施。</span>&nbsp;</p><p style="text-indent: -36pt; margin-left: 36pt; tab-stops: list 36.0pt" class="a6">　　<span lang="EN-US"><span>6.2.2<span style="font: 7pt 'Times New Roman'">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; </span></span></span><span style="font-family: 黑体">应急物资储备设施</span>&nbsp;</p><p style="text-indent: 15.75pt; margin-left: 5.25pt" class="a4">　　应根据避难场所容纳的人数和生活时间，<span>在应急避难场所内或周边设置</span>储备<span>应急生活物资的设施。</span>&nbsp;</p><p style="text-indent: 0cm; margin-left: 21pt; tab-stops: 21.0pt" class="af0">　　<span>应</span>利用应急避难场所内或周边的饭店、商店、超市、药店、仓库等进行应急物资储备。</p><p style="text-indent: 15.75pt; margin-left: 5.25pt" class="a4">　　<span>场所周边的应急物资储备设施与地震应急避难场所的距离应小于<span lang="EN-US">500 m</span>。</span>&nbsp;</p><p style="text-indent: -36pt; margin-left: 36pt; tab-stops: list 36.0pt" class="a6">　　<span lang="EN-US"><span>6.2.3<span style="font: 7pt 'Times New Roman'">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; </span></span></span><span style="font-family: 黑体">应急指挥管理设施</span>&nbsp;</p><p style="tab-stops: 21.0pt" class="af0">　　应设置广播、图像监控、有线<span class="msoIns"><ins datetime="2008-10-27T17:33" cite="mailto:ibm">通信</ins></span>、无线通信等应急管理设施。</p><p style="text-indent: 21pt" class="a4">　　广播系统应覆盖地震应急避难场所。</p><p style="text-indent: 21pt" class="a4">　　图像监控范围应覆盖应急篷宿区和地震应急避难场所内的道路。</p><p style="text-indent: 0cm; margin-left: 0cm; tab-stops: 21.0pt" class="af0">　　<span style="font-family: 黑体" lang="EN-US">6.3</span><span lang="EN-US"><span>&nbsp;&nbsp; </span></span>综合设施配置</p><p style="text-indent: 21pt; margin-left: 0cm; tab-stops: 21.0pt" class="af0">　　在基本、一般设施的基础上增加以下设施。&nbsp;</p><p style="text-indent: -36.75pt; margin-left: 36.75pt; tab-stops: list 36.75pt" class="af0">　　<span style="font-family: 黑体" lang="EN-US"><span>6.3.1<span style="font: 7pt 'Times New Roman'">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; </span></span></span><span style="font-family: 黑体">应急停车场</span>&nbsp;</p><p style="text-indent: 0cm; margin-left: 0cm; tab-stops: 21.0pt" class="af0">　　<span lang="EN-US"><span>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; </span></span>应急避难场所附近应设置应急车辆停车场。</p><p style="text-indent: -36.75pt; margin-left: 36.75pt; tab-stops: list 36.75pt" class="af0">　　<span style="font-family: 黑体" lang="EN-US"><span>6.3.2<span style="font: 7pt 'Times New Roman'">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; </span></span></span><span style="font-family: 黑体">应急停机坪</span>&nbsp;</p><p style="text-indent: 21pt; margin-left: 0cm; tab-stops: 21.0pt" class="af0">　　<span>应急避难场所内或周边应设置供直升机起降的应急停机坪。</span>&nbsp;</p><p style="text-indent: 21pt" class="a4">　　应急停机坪地面应平坦硬质，周围无高大建（构）筑物，保证直升机有升空平行安全角度。</p><p style="text-indent: -36.75pt; margin-left: 36.75pt; tab-stops: list 36.75pt" class="af0">　　<span style="font-family: 黑体" lang="EN-US"><span>6.3.3<span style="font: 7pt 'Times New Roman'">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; </span></span></span><span style="font-family: 黑体">应急洗浴设施</span>&nbsp;</p><p style="text-indent: 0cm; margin-left: 0cm; tab-stops: 21.0pt" class="af0">　　<span lang="EN-US"><span>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; </span></span>应结合应急厕所设置，增加洗浴功能<span>或设立可移动式洗浴设施。</span>&nbsp;</p><p style="text-indent: -36.75pt; margin-left: 36.75pt; tab-stops: list 36.75pt" class="af0">　　<span style="font-family: 黑体" lang="EN-US"><span>6.3.4<span style="font: 7pt 'Times New Roman'">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; </span></span></span><span style="font-family: 黑体">应急通风设施</span>&nbsp;</p><p style="text-indent: 21pt; margin-left: 0cm; tab-stops: 21.0pt" class="af0">　　通风条件有限的室内地震应急避难场所，应增设通风设施。</p><p style="text-indent: -36.75pt; margin-left: 36.75pt; tab-stops: list 36.75pt" class="af0">　　<span style="font-family: 黑体" lang="EN-US"><span>6.3.5<span style="font: 7pt 'Times New Roman'">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; </span></span></span><span style="font-family: 黑体">应急功能介绍设施</span>&nbsp;</p><p style="text-indent: 21pt; margin-left: 0cm; tab-stops: 21.0pt" class="af0">　　<span>应设置功能介绍图板，宜设置触摸屏、电子屏幕等设施。</span>&nbsp;</p><p style="text-indent: 0cm; margin: 7.8pt 0cm" class="a5">　　<a style="color: #000000" name="SectionMark5"><span lang="EN-US"><span>7　</span></span>其他要求</a>&nbsp;</p><p style="text-indent: 0cm; margin-left: 0cm" class="a6">　　<span><span style="font-family: 黑体" lang="EN-US"><span>7.1　</span></span><span style="font-family: 黑体">标识设置要求</span></span>&nbsp;</p><p style="text-indent: 21pt; margin-left: 0cm" class="a6">　　<span><span style="font-family: 宋体">场所周边主干道、路口应设置指示标<span class="msoIns"><ins datetime="2008-10-27T17:36" cite="mailto:ibm">志</ins></span>。</span></span>&nbsp;</p><p style="text-indent: 21pt; margin-left: 0cm" class="a6">　　<span><span style="font-family: 宋体">场所出入口应设置避难场所主标<span class="msoIns"><ins datetime="2008-10-27T17:36" cite="mailto:ibm">志</ins></span>。</span></span>&nbsp;</p><p style="text-indent: 21pt" class="a4">　　<span>场所内主要通道路口应设置应急设置的指示标<span class="msoIns"><ins datetime="2008-10-27T17:36" cite="mailto:ibm">志</ins></span>。</span>&nbsp;</p><p style="text-indent: 21pt; margin-left: 0cm" class="a6">　　<span><span style="font-family: 宋体">场所内</span></span><span><span style="font-family: 宋体">各类配套设施</span></span><span><span style="font-family: 宋体">应设</span></span><span><span style="font-family: 宋体">置明显的</span></span><span><span style="font-family: 宋体">标<span class="msoIns"><ins datetime="2008-10-27T17:36" cite="mailto:ibm">志</ins></span></span></span><span><span style="font-family: 宋体">。</span></span>&nbsp;</p><p style="text-indent: 0cm; margin-left: 0cm" class="a6">　　<span><span style="font-family: 黑体" lang="EN-US"><span>7.2　</span></span><span style="font-family: 黑体">抗震性能要求</span></span>&nbsp;</p><p style="text-indent: 21pt" class="a4">　　<span>地震应急避难场所内的建（构）筑物，以及利用周边建（构）筑作为配套设施用房的建筑，应达到<span lang="EN-US">GB 18306</span>－<span lang="EN-US">2001</span>规定的抗震设防要求。</span>&nbsp;</p><p style="text-indent: 0cm; margin-left: 0cm" class="a6">　　<span><span style="font-family: 黑体" lang="EN-US"><span>7.3　</span></span><span style="font-family: 黑体">篷宿区内分区要求</span></span>&nbsp;</p><p style="text-indent: 21pt; margin-left: 0cm" class="a6">　　<span><span style="font-family: 黑体">应急篷宿区应进行分区，</span></span><span><span style="font-family: 宋体">每个应急篷宿分区不应超过</span></span><span><span lang="EN-US">1000m</span></span><span><sup><span lang="EN-US">2</span></sup></span><span><span style="font-family: 宋体">；每个应急篷宿区之间间距应有大于</span></span><span><span lang="EN-US">2 m</span></span><span><span style="font-family: 宋体">的人行道。</span></span>&nbsp;</p><p style="text-indent: 0cm; margin-left: 0cm" class="a6">　　<span><span style="font-family: 黑体" lang="EN-US"><span>7.4　</span></span><span style="font-family: 黑体">无障碍要求</span></span>&nbsp;</p><p style="text-indent: 21pt" class="a4">　　<span><span>各类设施应考虑无障碍要求，按照<span lang="EN-US">JGJ 50</span>－<span lang="EN-US">2001</span>的规定设置。</span></span>&nbsp;</p><p style="text-indent: 0cm; margin-left: 0cm" class="a6">　　<span><span style="font-family: 黑体" lang="EN-US"><span>7.5　</span></span><span style="font-family: 黑体">功能介绍要求</span></span>&nbsp;</p><p style="text-indent: 21pt" class="a4">　　<span><span>入口处要设置标有文字说明的地震应急避难场所平面图和周边居民疏散路线图。</span></span>&nbsp;</p><p style="text-indent: 0cm" class="a4">　　<span><span lang="EN-US">7.6 </span>场址有效面积要求</span>&nbsp;</p><p style="text-indent: 21pt" class="a4">　　<span>应扣除场地内水域占地面积，大于<span lang="EN-US">7</span></span><span><span lang="EN-US">°</span>的陡坡占地面积，文物古迹保护占地面积，</span><span><span>以及建（构）筑物倒塌影响<span class="msoIns"><ins datetime="2008-10-27T17:42" cite="mailto:ibm">的</ins></span>面积</span>。</span>&nbsp;</p><p style="text-indent: 0cm" class="a4">　　<span><span lang="EN-US">&nbsp;</span></span>&nbsp;</p><p style="text-indent: 21pt" class="a4">　　<span><span lang="EN-US">&nbsp;</span></span>&nbsp;</p><p style="text-align: center; text-indent: 0cm" class="a4" align="center">　　<span>参<span lang="EN-US"><span>&nbsp; </span></span>考<span lang="EN-US"><span>&nbsp; </span></span>文<span lang="EN-US"><span>&nbsp; </span></span>献</span>&nbsp;</p><p
